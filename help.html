🆘 Help & Report Center

Get help and report online issues safely

If you experience any online problem such as cyberbullying, scams, or hacking, it is important to act quickly and ask for help from trusted people or platforms.

Talk to a Trusted Adult

Always inform parents, teachers, or guardians about online issues.

Report on Platform

Use built-in report tools on social media or apps.

Learn How

Block Users

Stop communication with anyone who is harmful or suspicious.

Save Evidence

Take screenshots if something unsafe happens online.

Stay Calm

Do not panic — help is always available.

Emergency Help

If serious, contact local authorities or cybercrime units.
