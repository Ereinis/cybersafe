⚠️ Online Dangers

Understanding risks in the digital world

Online threats are everywhere. Knowing them helps you stay safe and avoid dangerous situations on the internet.

👤 Cyberbullying

Harmful behavior or harassment using digital platforms.

🎣 Phishing

Fake emails or websites that steal personal information.

💰 Online Scams

Fraudulent offers designed to trick users into losing money.

🦠 Malware & Viruses

Malicious software that damages or steals data from devices.

🆔 Identity Theft

Someone using your personal information without permission.

📰 Fake News

False information spread to mislead users online.
