📘 About Cyber Hygiene

Understanding digital safety in the modern world

What is Cyber Hygiene?

Cyber hygiene refers to the practices and habits that users follow to maintain good online security and protect their personal data. Just like physical hygiene keeps you healthy, cyber hygiene keeps your digital life safe.

Why is it Important?

In today’s digital world, we use the internet for communication, education, and entertainment. Without proper safety habits, users can become victims of hacking, scams, identity theft, and other cyber threats.

Key Principles

🔐 Strong Passwords

Use unique and complex passwords for every account.

🛡️ Privacy Protection

Do not share personal data with strangers online.

⚠️ Stay Alert

Avoid suspicious links, emails, and downloads.

🔄 Regular Updates

Keep software and devices updated for security fixes.
